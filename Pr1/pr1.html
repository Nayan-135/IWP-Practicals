About RBU College

Founded in 1995, RBU College has been at the forefront of higher education for over two decades. Our institution is committed to academic excellence, research, and community service.

Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, skills, and values that prepare them for successful careers and responsible citizenship.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized institution that transforms lives through innovative education and research.

Admissions Information

We welcome applications from motivated students who are eager to pursue excellence in their chosen fields.

Eligibility Criteria

Bachelor's Programs: Completion of higher secondary education with minimum 50% marks.

Master's Programs: Bachelor's degree in relevant field with minimum 55% marks.

PhD Programs: Master's degree with strong research background.

Application Process

Submit online application form with required documents. Entrance examination and interview will be conducted for shortlisted candidates.
